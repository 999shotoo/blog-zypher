# On Simplicity

There's something powerful about keeping things simple. Not minimalist necessarily, but simple.

## Less is More

The best solutions are often the ones that do exactly what they need to do and nothing more. This applies to:

- Code
- Design
- Writing
- Life

## The Problem with Complexity

Complexity creeps in when we're not looking. One feature leads to another, one line of code becomes a hundred, one simple idea becomes a tangled mess.

## Getting Back to Basics

Sometimes you need to strip everything away and start from scratch. That's what this blog is about.
